# FY2025 Capital Table Instructions

The board table must show:

- Eligible own funds, SCR, MCR, SCR coverage ratio, and MCR coverage ratio.
- SCR attribution by risk module: Insurance underwriting, Market, Counterparty credit, Operational, and Other / diversification allocation.
- Movement versus the signed FY2024 board note.
- Key unresolved classification or attribution issues.
- Whether dividend-capacity, capital target, solvency, and bonus wording is safe for the board.

Control rules:

1. The total eligible own funds, SCR, MCR, SCR coverage ratio and MCR coverage ratio must come from locked CAP_R4.
2. Risk-module attribution for the board requires combined Finance Control and Capital Risk approval.
3. Single-function views may be used as working views but must not be described as final module-level attribution.
4. The expected combined approval file is `CAP_ATTRIB_Q4_COMBINED_APPROVAL_v7.pdf`.
5. Post-year-end management actions, January asset trades, January model recalibration files, and unsigned dividend notes must not change the 31 December CAP_R4 position.
6. Intercompany capital-support recharges must be eliminated from external own funds and SCR movement commentary.
7. The subordinated debt coupon true-up is already included in CAP_R4 and must not be double counted.
8. No final dividend-capacity, capital target, solvency, bonus, or “no concerns” wording may be used unless the relevant FY2025 review is signed.
