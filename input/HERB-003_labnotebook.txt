Worth remembering — there's a good methods paper (Telle & Thines 2008, comparing DNA extraction protocols across very old herbarium specimens) that found some extraction kits recover way more amplifiable DNA from aged material than others. We're not using their exact protocol here since we're following the standard CTAB method already on file, but it might be worth revisiting if our yields keep being marginal on the oldest specimens.
