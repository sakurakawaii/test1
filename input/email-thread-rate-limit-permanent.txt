Subject: EventBridge rate limit update — permanent baseline change from November 1

All,

Following completion of the Q3 infrastructure capacity review, I'm confirming the following change to EventBridge delivery rate limits:

Effective **2026-11-01**, the default delivery rate limit will be permanently set to **1200 events/min per subscription**. This reflects additional gateway capacity that was provisioned last month and brings our delivery infrastructure in line with what we'd originally targeted for the platform's steady-state operation.

This supersedes the characterisation in the v2.1 release notes, which described the 1200/min limit as a temporary migration-window elevation only. That framing was accurate at the time of publication but is no longer the right way to describe it.

Practical implications:
- Services that requested the elevated limit specifically for parallel-run purposes now have it as their permanent baseline — no action needed.
- Services that have not yet requested it will see the elevated limit automatically from November 1 without needing to raise a request.
- Services building for the first time can design for 1200/min as their capacity floor.

I'll arrange for the spec and migration documentation to be updated ahead of November 1. If you have questions in the meantime, ask in #platform-eng.

James
