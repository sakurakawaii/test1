# Production Change Log
## Exhibition: "Echoes of Civilization: Art Across Time"
### Department: Exhibitions Fabrication & Design Coordination
### Document Status: Internal Working Log — Rolling Updates


---

## Week 1 — Kickoff and Initial Scope

- Fabrication kickoff meeting held with Exhibitions Design, Preparation, and Facilities. Confirmed six-gallery footprint (Galleries 1–6) as scoped by Curatorial's initial checklist.
- Wall panel sizing standard set at 36" x 24" for primary didactic panels, 18" x 12" for object labels. **[Note: this sizing was revised in Week 5 — see below.]**
- Typography: primary display font confirmed as the museum's standard serif (per Marketing's branding notes). Object labels to use the standard sans-serif house font at 14pt minimum.
- Target install date discussed as first full week of October, pending final loan confirmations from Registration.

### Action Items
- Preparation to begin mount fabrication planning for Gallery 1 and Gallery 3 objects, since those checklists are most stable.
- Facilities to confirm case availability for Gallery 4 (climate-controlled cases needed for Reliquary Casket and Manuscript Leaf).

---

## Week 2 — Accessibility Requests

- Interpretation and Accessibility Services submitted initial requests:
  - Tactile reproductions requested for at least one object per gallery — six total. Fabrication flagged this as ambitious given timeline; **revised down to three tactile reproductions** (one per "anchor" gallery: Gallery 1, Gallery 3, Gallery 5) after follow-up discussion.
  - Audio guide stops requested at 24 locations. This number was later adjusted — see Week 6.
  - Wheelchair-accessible viewing height confirmed at 34"–48" for all wall-mounted labels, consistent with prior exhibitions.
- Braille labeling: initial plan was to include Braille only on tactile reproduction labels. Accessibility Services later requested Braille on all primary didactic panels as well — **this expansion was still under budget review as of Week 4.**

---

## Week 3 — Gallery Sequencing Revision

- Curatorial requested a sequencing change: Relief Fragment, Assyrian (previously slated for Gallery 4) reassigned to Gallery 2 to better support the "Cradles of Civilization" narrative arc. Fabrication updated floor plan mockups accordingly.
- Gallery 6 footprint under discussion — Curatorial and Design debated whether Gallery 6 should be positioned as the exhibition's final gallery or moved earlier in the sequence to break up the chronological flow with a "modern echo" mid-tour. **Decision at this stage: keep Gallery 6 as final gallery**, pending further review.
- Donor panel copy first draft submitted by Development. Initial draft listed three lead sponsors; Development noted a fourth sponsor was in final negotiation and might need to be added before print.

### Action Items
- Design to hold donor panel layout flexible enough to accommodate a possible fourth sponsor logo.
- Fabrication to proceed with floor plan based on current sequencing, understanding Gallery 6 placement may still shift.

---

## Week 4 — Typography and Signage Adjustments

- Marketing's proposed custom "ancient inscription" typeface for section headers was formally rejected by Design for accessibility/legibility reasons (consistent with Marketing's own notes). Standard serif confirmed as final for headers.
- Entrance signage color scheme: terracotta and gold approved for entrance and title wall only, not full exhibition identity, per joint decision with Marketing.
- Wayfinding map production paused pending final gallery names from Curatorial (Gallery 2 and Gallery 6 names still unresolved at this stage).

---

## Week 5 — Wall Panel Resizing

- Following a walk-through with Curatorial, primary didactic panel size was **revised from 36" x 24" to 30" x 20"** to better fit the reduced wall footprint in Gallery 4 (climate-controlled cases take up more floor space than originally planned). This size was applied retroactively to panels already drafted for Galleries 1–3 to maintain visual consistency across the exhibition.
- Object label size unchanged at 18" x 12".
- Preparation flagged that the Manuscript Leaf's case will require a custom low-light housing; Facilities confirmed compatible case is available but will need retrofitting, adding approximately one week to the install timeline for Gallery 4.

---

## Week 6 — Accessibility Finalization (Partial)

- Audio guide stop count finalized at 18 (revised down from the original 24 requested in Week 2), based on Interpretation's assessment of pacing and object density per gallery.
- Braille-on-all-panels request from Week 2 was **approved** after budget review, contingent on securing supplemental funding. Fabrication to proceed with Braille production for all primary didactic panels.
- Tactile reproduction count remains at three (Galleries 1, 3, 5) as revised in Week 2.

---

## Week 7 — Donor Panel Revision

- Development confirmed the fourth sponsor. Donor panel layout revised to accommodate four logos instead of three. Design updated panel proof; awaiting final sign-off from Development and Communications.
- A placement question arose: should the donor panel be positioned at the entrance (Gallery 1 threshold) or near the exit (Gallery 6)? Current working decision favors entrance placement, consistent with prior exhibitions, though Development has asked to revisit this once final sponsor tier is confirmed.

---

## Week 8 — Gallery 6 Sequencing Decision Revisited

- After further discussion, Curatorial reversed the Week 3 decision: Gallery 6 ("Modern Echoes," name still not finalized with Marketing) will now be positioned as the **penultimate** gallery, with a newly proposed reflective/seating area serving as the final stop instead. This supersedes the Week 3 note that Gallery 6 would remain the final gallery.
- Fabrication requested two weeks' lead time to update floor plans and signage routing accordingly. Design confirmed this is feasible within the current install timeline, though it will compress the buffer previously built in for late changes.
- Wayfinding maps still on hold pending Gallery 2 and Gallery 6 naming resolution from Curatorial/Marketing.

---

## Week 9 — Late Design Approvals

- Final approval received for Gallery 1 and Gallery 3 wall panel proofs at the revised 30" x 20" size.
- Gallery 4 case retrofit confirmed complete; installation for that gallery can proceed on the adjusted schedule established in Week 5.
- Donor panel (four-sponsor version) approved by Development, pending final placement decision from Week 7 discussion.
- Target install date holding at first full week of October, though Fabrication has noted this is now a tighter timeline than originally scoped in Week 1 given the Gallery 6 sequencing change and Gallery 4 case retrofit delay.

### Open Items Carried Forward
- Final donor panel placement (entrance vs. exit) — awaiting Development's confirmation of sponsor tier.
- Gallery 2 and Gallery 6 final names — blocking wayfinding map production.
- Braille panel production funding — contingent on supplemental budget approval.
- Confirm whether compressed timeline from Gallery 6 resequencing affects overall install date.
